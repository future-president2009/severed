## Why these aren't from Wikimedia

Three of the forty sites do not have free-license photo coverage on the open web. Wikimedia Commons doesn't have them, the Wikipedia articles have no embedded photos, and Openverse/Flickr-CC searches return either wrong sites or non-redistributable results. Direct permission from the photographers who have documented these sites was the right path — and worked.

The remaining 37 images are sourced from Wikimedia Commons under free licenses (CC BY, CC BY-SA, Public Domain, GFDL) and credited in `data/severed-data.json`.
